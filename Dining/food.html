LA BRASSERIE

Evoke in an intimate entertaining vibe, La Brasserie offers wide International buffet stations for breakfast and dinner, interactive live cooking stations featuring chef's dishes of the day. Featuring delicacies made from fresh ingredients, the menu offers International cuisine which balances healthy eating of tasty global-inspired dishes.

The restaurant is also available for private groups where you can entertain up to 60 guests.

MINI COURT

Ming Court offers Chinese cuisine with a Taiwanese touch and a wide selection of delectable hand-crafted Dim Sum All-you-can-eat.

The restaurant consists of two private dining rooms, one room seats 12 guests and one larger room with living room that can accommodate up to 25 diners.



THE LOUNGE

Conveniently located in a cozy nook of our grand lobby, The Lounge is a pampering venue where guests can indulge a leisurely drink while relaxing in a high ceiling ambiance.

Delectable snacks, afternoon tea and drinks are served throughout the day with live music in the evenings. The Lounge is an ideal place to unwind after a busy day in Saigon.
